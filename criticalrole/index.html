Well, I'm glad you asked! Dungeons & Dragons is a tabletop role-playing game wherein a group of players each make up a character they want to play and then the group goes on adventures together, often in a Tolkein-esque universe. In fact some of the races and classes are often those you find in the Lord of the Rings world: dwarves, elves, halflings, humans, orcs, and goblins who are rangers, fighters, wizards, druids, etc.

Critical Role is "a group of nerdy-ass voice actors who get together to play Dungeons & Dragons" and stream it online for others to watch. Sound crazy? Maybe it is but it's gathered a cult following since it first went live on March 12, 2015 and many credit this rag-tag group with Dungeons & Dragons' huge resurgence in popularity in recent years.

Critical Role streams on Twitch, with episodes made available on Youtube the following week.
